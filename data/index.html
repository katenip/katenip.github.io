Food Establishment Risk Dashboard

power bi • dax

Decision-focused dashboard built from Mecklenburg County restaurant inspection records. The layout separates current inspection performance from predicted decline to reduce misinterpretation.

  • Created a risk indicator using custom DAX measures combining recent scores and trends.
  • Added geospatial mapping to identify clusters of higher-risk establishments.
  • Iterated on labeling/color after feedback to improve readability for non-technical users.

usability
