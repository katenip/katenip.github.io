Food Establishment Risk Dashboard

power bi • dax

Dashboard built from Mecklenburg County inspection records. It separates current inspection performance from predicted decline to keep decisions clear.

  • Custom DAX measures combine recent scores and trends into a risk indicator.
  • Geospatial mapping highlights clusters of higher-risk establishments.
  • Labels/colors were adjusted based on feedback to improve readability.
